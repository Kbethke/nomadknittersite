About Million on Mars

You are the Executive Directory of Delta Colony, one of several colonies with seed funding from Leon Dusk, the Chairman of ZeeSpace, Inc.

Initially funded with $10 Billion dollars, your mission is to colonize Mars and ultimately drive the population to 1 million people.

You are be responsible for making all the strategic decisions on how to build the colony, starting from what the first rover should mine.

Game Phases

1. Scout the colony location with orbiters and some robotic landers

* Will you look for maximum water ice?
* Or are you looking for rare ores?
* Near the equator?
* Deep in Valles Marineris?

2. Go Shopping!

* Load up your BFR with tools, supplies and equipment to fund your colony:
* How many rovers will you bring?
* How many kilowatts of solar?
* Do you bring a nuclear reactor?
* Keep on eye on how much mass and volume as you load up your BFR
* Oh! And all that cool stuff costs $$$

3. Land your (first) BFR, and start the robotic build-out of the colony.

* How quickly can you get your Sabatier reactor running?
* Send additional BFRs as needed - but watch your $ and your time as you are competing against the progress from Alpha, Beta and Gamma Colonies.

4. Send in the first 12 humans explorers. Then the next 100 humans

* DO you have back up systems for the core life support functions?
* Remeber crops take about 90 days to harvest time, make sure you have plenty of food for your explorers

5. Build up the colony - 1,000 and then 10,000! (start to charge for passage) (automation at scale)

* Time to start scaling up the colony
* Reclamation on great scale
* And time to start charging the colonists for their passage to the *Newest World* (and re-fill the badly drained budget)

6. Million on Mars

* Self-sustaining One Million people on Mars is the win
* are you a winner at strategic planning?
